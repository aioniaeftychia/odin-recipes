Fruit Salad

Description

This fruit salad recipe will change your life. It's easy to make, and even easier to enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Mangoes
  • Pineapples
  • Spanspek
  • Honey Melon
  • Strawberries
  • Raspberries
  • Yogurt

Steps

  1. Chop fruits
  2. Add Yogurt
  3. Mix
  4. Chill for 30 minutes
  5. Serve and enjoy!
