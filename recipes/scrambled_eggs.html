Scrambled Eggs

Description

This scrambled eggs recipe will change your life. It's easy to make, and even easier to enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • Cream
  • Salt
  • Parsley
  • Butter
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk until yellow and white are mixed.
  2. Add a splash of cream, and a pinch of salt
  3. Mix until incorporated
  4. Heat pan at medium heat for a few minutes
  5. Add a soon of butter to pan and wait until butter stops foaming
  6. Add egg mixture
  7. Stir continuously until eggs are fluffy but still creamy
  8. Remove from heat and serve on toast
  9. Chop some parsley and sprinkle on top
